About Me

I graduated from Temple University with a Bachelor of Science in Environmental Geochemistry. My journey into Geographic Information Systems (GIS) began during my senior year when I took a GIS and Remote Sensing course and fell in love with the field. This passion led me to pursue a Professional Science Master's program in GIS at Temple University, where I am currently a year into my studies. I find great fulfillment in the work I'm doing, particularly in exploring the intersections of public and environmental health, community development, and policy-making, with a focus on addressing discrimination faced by minority populations. I am eager to apply my skills and knowledge to effect positive change in these areas and am actively seeking opportunities, whether it be a job or internship, that will allow me to further expand my experience and make a meaningful impact.
